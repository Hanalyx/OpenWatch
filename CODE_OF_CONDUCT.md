# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

The OpenWatch project is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and
harassment-free experience for everyone who participates, regardless of
background or identity. We expect all contributors, maintainers, and community
members to act professionally and considerately in every project space.

## Our standard

OpenWatch has adopted the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**, as its code of
conduct. It defines the behavior we expect, the behavior we do not tolerate, the
responsibilities of maintainers, and the scope of enforcement.

Please read the full text:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

By taking part in this project you agree to uphold it.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it confidentially to
the maintainers at **contact@hanalyx.com**. Reports are reviewed promptly and
handled discreetly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and safety
of anyone who reports an issue.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and
may take any action they deem appropriate, up to and including a temporary or
permanent ban from project spaces, in response to behavior that violates it. The
Contributor Covenant's enforcement guidelines describe the escalation steps we
follow.
